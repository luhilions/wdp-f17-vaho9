My Favorite Animal

Water Bear.

Tardigrades also known as water bears, space bears, or moss piglets. They are water-dwelling, eight-legged, segmented micro-animals. They were first discovered by the German zoologist Johann August Ephraim Goeze in 1773. They can survive in tempertures 1 K (−458 °F; −272 °C) (close to absolute zero) and 420 K (300 °F; 150 °C) They can also surivive in space and in the dephs of the ocean.
